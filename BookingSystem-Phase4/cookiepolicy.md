# Cookie Policy

**Last updated:** 18 December 2025

---

## 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites function properly and securely.

---

## 2. Cookies We Use

The Service uses **only strictly necessary cookies**. We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking cookies.

| Cookie            | Purpose                                                             | Type               | Retention                             |
| ----------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------ | ------------------------------------- |
| Session cookie    | Maintains your authenticated session and enables core functionality | Strictly necessary | Deleted when the browser session ends |
| CSRF token cookie | Protects the Service against cross-site request forgery attacks     | Strictly necessary | Deleted when the browser session ends |

## 3. Legal Basis

The use of these cookies is necessary for the proper functioning and security of the Service. Under the GDPR and ePrivacy rules, strictly necessary cookies do **not** require user consent.
